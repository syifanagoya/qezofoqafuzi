Oulye Ndoye: Who is Raphael Warnocks ex-wife?

Oulèye Ndoye is a famous health leader in human rights, social media influencer, and philanthropist. She is famous as the ex-wife of Raphel Warnock. She was born in 1985.

Ndoye also works as a global health and human rights policy advisor.

She has received numerous scholarships and fellowships to live, work, study and teach in the field of human rights in Africa, Asia and the Middle East.

Ndoye also served as the inaugural Senior Human Trafficking Fellow, a member of the Mayor of Atlanta’s executive cabinet, where she collaborated closely with The Partnership for Freedom.

Ndoye married Raphel on February 14, 2016. The couple separated in November 2019, and their divorce was finalised in 2020. They have two children together.
