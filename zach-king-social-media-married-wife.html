Who Is Zach King? Social Media, &amp; Details On Married Life With Wife

Zach King is one of the prominent social media stars. Who Is Zach King?

Zach King is an American social media star, who is best known for his YouTube content. He was born in 1990 in Portland, Orlando, in the United States and he celebrates his birthday on 4 February.

He grew up with his three sisters, Katie, Anne, and another one, whose identity is not disclosed. Katie is his adopted sister. Besides, his parent's details and family background also lies in mystery.

Zach King was homeschooled from a young age until he joined Biola University in La Mirada, California from where he graduated with a major in Cinema and Media Arts.

Concerning the body measurements of the 30-year-old YouTuber, he stands at a decent height of 6 feet and weighs 80 kg or 176 lbs in weight.

Zach King On Social Media Platforms

Zach King has made his prominent strong and wide on social media platforms. He is one of the most followed personalities on Tik Tok, where he has garnered more than 49.5 million followers, as of now. Similarly, he has over 570.7 million likes on the platform.

Besides that, Zach King has 23.8 million followers on his Instagram account. Moreover, his self-titled YouTube channel enriches the subscribers of 8.37 million, as of September 2020. He posts several videos of pranks, lip-syncs, and also features celebrities on his Tik Tok contents. He has collaborated with the celebs like Selena Gomez and others. Moreover, King posts the cooking tutorials on his Tik Tok.

His Youtube channel, which he created back on 15 October 2013 features the videos of pranks, social experiments, and others.

Who Is Zach King's Wife?

Zach King is married to his wife, Rachel Holm, for nearly six years now. He and Rachel Holm shared their wedding vows back on 28 December 2014 and have been together since then. The lovebirds were in a relationship for one and a half years at the time of their wedding. After nearly four years of their marriage, the lovely duo welcomed their son in August 2018, who is now two years old.

Similarly, Zach King and Rachel Holm are parents of their son named Mason, whom they adopted in April 2019.
