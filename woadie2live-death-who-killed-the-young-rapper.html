Woadie2live Death: Who Killed The Young Rapper?

Young rapper, Woadie2live, died at the age of 23. The rapper was shot to death on the 5th of September, 2021. Woadie2live was shot in the neck and arm, and died from a fatal shot to his neck.

Initially, it was disregarded because a lot of people thought it was a hoax until sympathies came pouring out from loved ones, including fellow rapper, Asian Doll, and his manager.

It is alleged that his death was caused by his close friend, Bailey Philips also known as Bandman Fari, a fellow rapper who has appeared in some of his music videos. It is also alleged that he killed Woadie2live because he was jealous of his friend’s success. And this jealousy caused him to shoot his friend in front of a club.

Woadie2live’s death follows a series of rappers dying in the past months.  Woadie2live is known for popular songs like “New Oops”, “Whole lotta love”, “Extortion”, etc. He shot to fame after becoming a member of Dalworth. And he has gained a large fan base who support his music.
