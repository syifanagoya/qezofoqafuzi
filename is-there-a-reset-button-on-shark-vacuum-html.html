Is there a reset button on shark vacuum?

Answer

The following are the actions to take in order to reset the shark vacuum: Set the Power button to the On/Off position and leave the vacuum unplugged until you are ready to use it. Is there a fuse in a shark vacuum cleaner?

It’s possible that the problem isn’t with the cord or your ability to plug the device into the wall. It is possible that your Shark vacuum is not turning on because a fuse has blown or a circuit breaker has been tripped.
