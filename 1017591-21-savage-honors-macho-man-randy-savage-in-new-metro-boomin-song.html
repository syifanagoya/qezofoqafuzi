21 Savage Honors Macho Man Randy Savage In New Metro Boomin Song

21 Savage is featured on three songs in the new Metro Boomin album NOT ALL HEROES WEAR CAPES, and on “10 Freaky Girls” he pays homage to one of the greatest professional wrestlers of all-time. “Bodyslam a n***a like I’m Randy,” raps 21 after bringing up Sandy Cheeks from Spongebob Squarepants, the alcoholic beverage brandy, and talking about a girl eating molly “like it’s candy” earlier in the song.

Give a listen to the song below:

The latest album from Metro Boomin is full of bangers, and features the likes of Young Thug, Travis Scott, and Drake on its 13 tracks. Also, while it has nothing to do with “Macho Man” Randy Savage, I must mention that 21 Savage raps the line “Low self-esteem, he on BlackPeopleMeet” on the track “Don’t Come Out The House.” Issa punchline.
