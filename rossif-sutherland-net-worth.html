What is Rossif Sutherland's Net Worth?

Rossif Sutherland is a Canadian actor who has a net worth of $4 million. Rossif Sutherland was born in Vancouver, British Columbia in September 1978. He is the son of actor Donald Sutherland and the brother of actor and producer Angus Sutherland as well as the half-brother of actor Kiefer Sutherland. Rossif Sutherland starred as Lester Kertzenstein on the television series ER from 2003 to 2004. In 2012 he starred as Pen Martin on the TV series King.

Sutherland starred as Nostradamus on the series Reign from 2013 to 2015. He has appeared in several films including Timeline, Red Doors, I'm Reed Fish, Poor Boy's Game, High Life, The Con Artist, I'm Yours, Dead Before Dawn, Hellions, River, Edge of Winter, and more. He has also appeared in episodes of the TV series Monk, Crossing Lines, Cracked, Haven, The Expanse, and others. Sutherland has been nominated for a Genie Award and a Canadian Screen Award.
