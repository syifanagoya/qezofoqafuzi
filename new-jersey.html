Pricing, offer and terms subject to change at any time. Data options may be switched at any time during bill cycle. Downgrade limited to 1x per bill cycle and effective with following bill cycle. Upgrades effective immediately. International talk/text from U.S. and talk/text/data while traveling abroad not included.

Quality of service (QoS): Optimum Mobile leverages T-Mobile 4G LTE and 5G networks to deliver nationwide network coverage. After reaching 1 GB or 3 GB data cap, data is reduced to 2G (128 Kbps) speeds for remainder of bill cycle. Additional data purchases available once 1 GB/3 GB data cap is reached. After reaching 20 GB (for Unlimited data plans) or 50 GB (for Unlimited Max data plans) data is reduced to 3G (512 Kbps) speeds for remainder of bill cycle. Subject to this QoS policy, tethering at 600 Kbps and video streaming at SD quality (about 480p), for by the Gig plans, and after 5 GB of tethered data usage for Unlimited Data plan. For Unlimited Max plan, speeds will be reduced to 3G (512 Kbps) after 15 GB of tethered data usage in a month for the remainder of the billing period, and streaming video will be at HD quality (720p). Domestic off-net (roaming) at 2G speeds. See our Broadband Disclosure for additional performance and network management details.

Non-Internet Customer Surcharge: Displayed pricing for Optimum Internet customers. Starting with third billing cycle, a surcharge of $20/mo per line will be added unless Optimum Internet account is linked to mobile account.
