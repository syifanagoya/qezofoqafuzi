Was Michael Jackson friends with Chris Tucker?

Chris Tucker is an American comedian and actor who won the 2002 Kids’ Choice Award for Favorite Movie Actor and MTV Movie Award for Best Fight in 2002. Chris Tucker appeared in the HBO comedy series dubbed “Def Comedy Jam” in the early 1990s.

Chris Tucker is 50 years of age and he served as the host during the 2013 BET Awards.

Michael Jackson was an American dancer, singer and songwriter who was regarded as one of the best singers of the 20th century. Michael Jackson was named “The King of Pop” and he is the most awarded music artist of all time even though he died over a decade ago.

Michael Jackson was born on August 29, 1958, and died on June 25, 2009, at the age of 50 years.

Was Michael Jackson friends with Chris Tucker?

Michael Jackson and Chris Tucker were very good friends and their friendship is well-document. Chris Tucker mostly spent time and hung out with Michael Jackson at the Neverland Ranch which was owned by Michael Jackson.
