Rob Van Dams Reaction To His Return To WWE, Cena Comments on Victory Over Mark Henry

WWE.com caught up with Rob Van Dam after the brutal All Stars Money in the Bank Ladder Match. He said the reaction that he got from the crowd in Philadelphia was awesome. He also said there is much more to come from "Mr. Monday Night."

John Cena was interviewed backstage after his match with Mark Henry last night. Cena talked about many challenges in his career, but this one was the most difficult.
